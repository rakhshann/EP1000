Final Image

We were tasked to choose a photo of ourselves, remove the background of the photo and substitute the background with a seaside scene. To do so, I first used the 'Free Select Tool' to trace the image of myself. Then, I inverted the selection and removed the background by making it transparent. Afterwards, I added the image of the seaside by making it another layer. And the final product is an image of me looking like I am talking a walk by the seaside.
