Design

This is the design for the case to house the ultrasonic sensor. There are holes to allow the transmiter and receiver to not be blocked. There is also another hole to ensure that the sensor's pins can be accessed.(Wires can also feel out of this hole). I have added the first 3 letters of my name in the 'bauhaus' font to the top of the casing.

This is the design for the case to house the arduino NANO. There are holes to allow the wires to come out of it as well as holes to allow the usb to be plugged in without having to remove the arduino NANO.
